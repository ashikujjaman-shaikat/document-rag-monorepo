========================================
        HR POLICY DOCUMENT
        Acme Corporation
        Effective Date: January 1, 2025
========================================

1. RECRUITMENT & HIRING
   - All open positions must be posted internally for a minimum of 5 business days.
   - Background checks are mandatory for all new hires.
   - Offer letters must be approved by the HR Manager before issuance.

2. WORKING HOURS
   - Standard working hours: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
   - Employees are entitled to a 1-hour lunch break.
   - Overtime must be pre-approved by the department head.

3. LEAVE POLICY
   - Annual Leave    : 18 days per year
   - Sick Leave      : 10 days per year
   - Casual Leave    : 5 days per year
   - Maternity Leave : 16 weeks (as per applicable law)
   - Paternity Leave : 2 weeks

4. CODE OF CONDUCT
   - Employees must maintain a professional and respectful work environment.
   - Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.
   - Confidential company information must not be disclosed to external parties.

5. PERFORMANCE REVIEW
   - Performance appraisals are conducted bi-annually (June & December).
   - Employees will receive written feedback and set goals for the next cycle.
   - Promotions and salary increments are tied to performance ratings.

6. TERMINATION
   - Either party may terminate employment with 30 days' written notice.
   - Gross misconduct may result in immediate termination without notice.
   - All company property must be returned on or before the last working day.

7. GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE
   - Employees may raise grievances in writing to the HR department.
   - HR will acknowledge the grievance within 3 business days.
   - Resolution will be sought within 15 business days of acknowledgment.

========================================
  Approved by: HR Director, Acme Corp.
========================================
