========================================
        COMPANY RULES & REGULATIONS
        Acme Corporation
        Version: 2025-01
========================================
 
1. GENERAL CONDUCT
   - All employees are expected to behave professionally at all times.
   - Punctuality is required; habitual tardiness may lead to disciplinary action.
   - Employees must wear their ID badges visibly while on company premises.
 
2. DRESS CODE
   - Business casual attire is required from Monday to Thursday.
   - Casual/smart casual is permitted on Fridays.
   - Client-facing days require formal business attire regardless of the day.
 
3. USE OF COMPANY RESOURCES
   - Company equipment (laptops, phones, vehicles) is for business use only.
   - Personal use of company internet is limited to break times and must be reasonable.
   - Unauthorized installation of software on company devices is prohibited.
 
4. SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY
   - Employees must not post confidential company information on social media.
   - Opinions shared online must not be presented as the company's official stance.
   - Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
 
5. WORKPLACE SAFETY
   - All employees must follow safety protocols and report hazards immediately.
   - Emergency exits must remain clear and unobstructed at all times.
   - Participation in mandatory safety drills is compulsory.
 
6. VISITORS & GUESTS
   - All visitors must sign in at the front desk and wear a visitor badge.
   - Employees are responsible for the conduct of their guests on premises.
   - Unauthorized access to restricted areas is strictly prohibited.
 
7. DISCIPLINARY ACTION
   Level 1 – Verbal Warning
   Level 2 – Written Warning
   Level 3 – Final Written Warning / Suspension
   Level 4 – Termination of Employment
 
   The severity of the offense determines the starting level of disciplinary action.
 
8. COMPLIANCE
   - All employees must read, understand, and sign acknowledgment of these rules.
   - Rules are subject to periodic revision; employees will be notified of changes.
   - Non-compliance may result in disciplinary measures as outlined above.
